Who You'll Learn From

At Student Startup Tribe, we’re building a dynamic network of mentors — from early-stage founders to industry veterans — committed to supporting student entrepreneurs. Our mentors share your passion, your ambition, and the belief that great startups can start on campus.

What to Expect from Mentorship

  • 1-on-1 Support: Personalized guidance tailored to your startup stage.
  • Live Q&A Sessions: Weekly meetups to learn from mentor journeys and expertise.
  • Pitch & Product Reviews: Improve your startup through practical feedback.
  • Networking Access: Get warm intros to founders, builders, and future collaborators.

We're Partnering With...

As a fresh community, we’re onboarding a diverse panel of startup enablers from across domains including:

  • Founders of bootstrapped & funded ventures
  • Product and growth leaders from top startups
  • Early-stage angel investors open to student-led ideas
  • University alumni with experience in building real companies
